vMobile™ Configuration Mode

vMobile™ app on Android / iPhone mobile device is used for configuring and controlling individual vMobile™ devices.
To set the vMobile™ in configuration mode, press Enter button on the vMobile™ hardware device.
vMobile™ is put to Access Point (AP) mode.

Control Options available on the (Step 1) Configuration screen
  • Back: click on the Back button in the app to navigate to the Home screen
  • Next: click on the Next button in the app to navigate to the next configuration (Step 2) screen
