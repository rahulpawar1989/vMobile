vMobile™ and vMobile App Connection

Step 2 Configuration is required to connect vMobile App to vMobile™ device required for initial configurations on vMobile™. Both vMobile App and vMobile™ are connected via vMobile device network.

Connect my vMobile to WiFi

Follow the steps below to establish connection between vMobile App and vMobile™ -

  • Tap the Connect my vMobile to Wi-Fi link available on the Step 2 Configuration screen. This redirects to Phone’s settings.
  • Use push button and enable the Wi-Fi on phone’s settings. Ignore if already enabled.
  • Select the vMobile Wi-Fi Network from the list of available networks. This prompts the Enter Password screen.
  • Enter the Password for the selected Wi-Fi network and connect to vMobile. Now, the vMobile App and the vMobile are in the same Wi-Fi network.
  • Once connected to vMobile Wi-Fi Network, navigate to the vMobile application by tapping on the phone’s back button.
Control Options available on the (Step 2) Configuration screen
  • Back: click on the Back button in the app to navigate to the Step 1 Configuration screen
  • Next: click on the Next button in the app to navigate to the next configuration (Step 3) screen
